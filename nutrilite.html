Section 01
The problem we're solving — and how
Nutrilite's distribution network has many levels — a top distributor, mid-level agents, and frontline sellers. Today, leads are lost in WhatsApp chats, reminders are forgotten, and nobody has a clear view of who is following up with whom. We fix all of this with a single WhatsApp agent that every level of the network can use — branded for Nutrilite, no app to download.

Section 03
Reminder set — AI auto-calls the prospect
Once a lead is logged, the system sets a follow-up reminder. At the scheduled time, our AI voice agent calls the prospect automatically — introducing Nutrilite, understanding their interest, and scheduling a callback or meeting with the agent. No manual dialling needed.
